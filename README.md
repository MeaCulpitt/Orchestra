Orchestra creates value through two deeply integrated functions:

### 1. The Lead Architect (Project Management)
The core product of Orchestra is the **Managed Intelligence Package**. Our Miners act as high-level General Contractors. When a user provides a complex objective, the Miner:
* **Decomposes** the goal into a strategic, multi-stage execution roadmap.
* **Procures** intelligence by programmatically hiring the best experts in the metagraph (e.g., chaining Hone for logic, Desearch for data, and Ridges for code).

### 2. The Subnet Ledger (The Standard of Record)
Effective management requires a perfect understanding of the workforce. The **Subnet Ledger** is a continuously updated, Pydantic-validated record of what every specialized subnet in the ecosystem actually produces. 
* **Naturally Maintained:** The Ledger is a byproduct of our miners' management work; to route tasks accurately, they must maintain the most accurate directory in the metagraph.
* **LLM Ready:** By transforming messy, unstructured data into deterministic, type-safe JSON, the Ledger acts as the "Rosetta Stone" for autonomous agents and LLMs.
