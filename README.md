**SN-Orchestra** is a coordination-layer subnet on the Bittensor network. We solve the "Fragmentation Gap" by providing a centralized neural hub that manages specialized decentralized agents across the ecosystem.

---

## üìñ Subnet Overview
In the 2026 decentralized AI landscape, intelligence is abundant but heavily siloed. Users currently interface with separate subnets for coding, data retrieval, and compute. **SN-Orchestra** introduces a new commodity to the network: **Orchestration Intelligence**.

Instead of performing raw compute, this subnet specializes in **Strategic Management**. It takes a single, complex user intent and intelligently delegates it across the 128+ subnets of Bittensor, synthesizing the results into one high-fidelity solution.

### Core Functions:
* **Intent Decomposition:** Breaking complex human prompts into a Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG) of actionable sub-tasks.
* **Expert Procurement:** Identifying and "hiring" the most reputable UIDs from specialized subnets (e.g., Code from SN62, Search from SN13).
* **Consensus Synthesis:** Aggregating disparate outputs and verifying cryptographic execution proofs to ensure a "Chain of Trust."

---

## üèóÔ∏è How it Works
The subnet utilizes a **Manager-Auditor** relationship to maintain the highest standards of decentralized logic:

1.  **Miners (The Managers):** These nodes are the "brains" of the operation. They receive tasks, create execution plans, pay for services on other subnets, and deliver the final synthesized product.
2.  **Validators (The Auditors):** These nodes are the "referees." They verify that miners are using legitimate expert nodes and that the final output is logically sound and accurate.
3.  **The Synapse:** All communication is handled via a standardized contract that ensures every task has a verifiable "paper trail" of cross-subnet execution.
